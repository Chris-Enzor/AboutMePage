About Me


Hello, I'm Chris Enzor. I'm currently enrolled at Coastal Carolina University; my major is Digital Culture and Design and I'll be graduating this semester. I have had plenty of inspiration from works by artists like Zdzisław Beksiński, Ivan Seal, Pablo Picasso, and Cam de Leon.

I'm planning on going into video game design as a career, but when I graduate I'll take any job I can get just to get started.

My aesthetic is typically either dark/ominous or colorful minimalism.
